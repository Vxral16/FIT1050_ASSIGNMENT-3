About Honash HC

Melbourne's most passionate university hockey community since 1962.

Our Story

Born from a shared love of the game among Monash University students, Honash Hockey Club has written its story one season at a time since 1962. What started as a handful of students kicking a ball around a borrowed pitch has grown into one of Melbourne's most respected university sporting clubs - 12 teams, 200+ members, and decades of memories on and off the field.

Our Mission

We believe hockey is more than a sport - it's a way of building friendships and a sense of belonging. Whether you've never held a stick or you're chasing a state championship - Honash HC is your club. We welcome players of every background and skill level. Stay for the community.

Why Join Honash HC?

Everything you need to play, grow, and belong.

Compete at Every Level

From social Saturday morning games to state-level competition, our 12 teams across mens, womens and mixed divisions mean there is a perfect fit for every player - no matter your experience. Step up. Show up. Play.

A Community That Lasts

Some of our most loyal members joined as first-year students and never left. The friendships you build at Honash HC extend well beyond the pitch - end-of-season trips, club socials, and a culture built on belonging.

Built Around Student Life

Training schedules that work around lectures, assignments, and exam periods. We've been a student club for over 60 years - we know how to make sport and study work together without sacrificing either.

Quality Coaching

Our coaching staff bring state and national-level experience to every session. Whether you're learning the basics or fine-tuning your game, you'll develop faster at Honash HC than anywhere else.
